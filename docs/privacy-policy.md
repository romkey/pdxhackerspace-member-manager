# Privacy Policy

**[Your Hackerspace Name]**
**Last Updated: [Date]**

This Privacy Policy describes how [Your Hackerspace Name] ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of our members, guests, and visitors. We are committed to transparency about our data practices and to protecting your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Member Account Information

When you become a member or create an account, we collect:

- **Identity information:** Full name, username, email address, additional email addresses, aliases, pronouns, and optional biography
- **Membership details:** Membership status, start date, membership plan, and dues status
- **Preferences:** Profile visibility settings, greeting preferences, and profile links you choose to add
- **Authentication credentials:** Password (stored as a cryptographic hash, never in plain text) or single sign-on identity through our authentication provider

### 1.2 Payment Information

We process membership dues and donations through third-party payment processors. We collect and store:

- **Transaction records:** Payment amounts, dates, currency, and transaction identifiers
- **Payer information:** Name and email address associated with each payment
- **Subscription status:** Whether your subscription is active, cancelled, or paused

We do **not** store credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or other sensitive financial credentials. That information is held solely by our payment processors.

### 1.3 Physical Access Data

To manage building access and security, we collect:

- **RFID card identifiers:** Unique codes associated with your physical access card
- **Access logs:** Records of when and where your access card is used (e.g., front door, specific equipment areas), including timestamps and the action performed
- **PIN codes:** Temporary PINs used during RFID enrollment, which expire and are deleted within five minutes

### 1.4 Training and Certification Records

We track equipment training to ensure member safety:

- **Training completions:** Which equipment you have been trained on, the date of training, and who provided the training
- **Trainer certifications:** If you are a certified trainer, the topics you are qualified to teach

### 1.5 Incident Reports

For the safety of all members, we maintain records of incidents that occur at the space:

- **Report details:** Date, type, description, and resolution of incidents
- **Involved members:** Names of members involved in or witnessing an incident
- **Supporting materials:** Photos or links related to the incident

### 1.6 Communication Data

We collect limited communication data in the course of membership management:

- **Email records:** Logs of automated emails sent to you (e.g., membership reminders, notifications), including delivery status
- **Slack profile information:** If you use our Slack workspace, we sync your Slack display name, username, and profile information to help identify members

### 1.7 Video Surveillance

Our facility is equipped with security cameras in both indoor common areas and exterior locations:

- **Video recordings:** Cameras record video footage of common areas, entrances, exits, and outdoor spaces
- **Purpose:** Video surveillance is used solely for the security and safety of members, guests, and the facility
- **Signage:** Areas under video surveillance are marked with visible signage

Cameras are **not** placed in restrooms, changing areas, or other spaces where there is a reasonable expectation of privacy.

### 1.8 Activity Logs

We maintain an audit trail of changes to member accounts:

- **Journal entries:** Records of significant account changes (e.g., membership status changes, training completions, payment linkages), including what changed, when, and who made the change

### 1.9 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party services you use in connection with your membership:

- **Payment processors** (PayPal, Recharge, Ko-fi): Transaction details and payer identity
- **Authentication provider** (Authentik): Account identity and group memberships
- **Slack:** Profile information and activity status

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Membership management:** Maintaining your account, tracking membership status and dues, and communicating with you about your membership
- **Access control:** Granting and managing physical access to the facility and equipment
- **Safety and security:** Monitoring facility access, responding to incidents, ensuring only trained members use certain equipment, and maintaining video surveillance of common areas
- **Payment processing:** Recording and reconciling membership dues and donations
- **Communication:** Sending membership-related notifications, reminders, and announcements
- **Compliance:** Meeting our obligations as a membership organization, including maintaining accurate records

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 3. Who Can Access Your Information

### 3.1 Administrators

Designated administrators of the organization can access member account information, payment records, access logs, training records, and incident reports as needed to manage the space.

### 3.2 Other Members

Depending on your profile visibility setting:

- **Public:** Your name and profile information are visible to anyone
- **Members only:** Your profile is visible only to logged-in members
- **Private:** Your profile is visible only to administrators

Your payment details, access logs, and incident involvement are **never** visible to other members.

### 3.3 Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with the following categories of service providers, solely for the purposes described in this policy:

- **Payment processors:** To process membership dues (PayPal, Recharge, Ko-fi)
- **Authentication provider:** To manage secure login (Authentik)
- **Communication platforms:** To facilitate member communication (Slack)
- **Hosting provider:** Our member management system is hosted on infrastructure that may process your data

These providers have their own privacy policies governing their handling of your information.

### 3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal process, protect the safety of any person, or protect our rights.

## 4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information:

- Passwords are stored using secure one-way hashing and are never stored in plain text
- Sensitive parameters (passwords, tokens, keys) are filtered from application logs
- Administrative access is restricted to authorized individuals
- Our member management system uses encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Temporary credentials (such as RFID enrollment PINs) are automatically deleted after five minutes

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Member accounts:** Your account information is retained for the duration of your membership and afterward for record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your account (see Section 7).
- **Payment records:** Transaction records are retained indefinitely for financial record-keeping and reconciliation.
- **Access logs:** Door and equipment access logs are retained indefinitely for security purposes.
- **Video surveillance:** Video footage is retained according to the capacity of our recording systems, typically overwriting the oldest footage as storage fills. Footage related to an active incident investigation may be preserved longer.
- **Incident reports:** Incident records are retained indefinitely for safety and organizational record-keeping.
- **Training records:** Training and certification records are retained indefinitely to ensure ongoing equipment safety compliance.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

Our member management system uses session cookies that are necessary for you to log in and use the system. We do **not** use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party tracking technologies.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** You can view your own profile, payment history, training records, and access logs through the member management system
- **Correction:** You can update your profile information at any time through the member management system, or by contacting an administrator
- **Profile visibility:** You can control who sees your profile by adjusting your profile visibility setting
- **Data export:** You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by contacting an administrator
- **Deletion:** You may request deletion of your account. Some information (such as payment records and incident reports) may be retained as required for legal, financial, or safety reasons
- **Objection:** You may object to specific uses of your data by contacting an administrator

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [contact email address].

## 8. Children's Privacy

Membership is not open to individuals under the age of 18 without parental or guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
