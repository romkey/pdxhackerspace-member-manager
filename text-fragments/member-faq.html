Frequently asked questions

These answers describe Member Manager in general. Your organization may use different names for menus or extra steps—ask a board member or manager if something looks different here.

How do I sign in?

Your space almost always uses single sign-on (for example OpenID Connect). Use the login link on the site, complete sign-in with your identity provider, and you land on the dashboard. Some spaces also offer RFID kiosk login or a one-time login link from your profile—only use options your admins have told you about.

What is the dashboard?

After you sign in, the home page summarizes useful information: membership status, messages, and shortcuts to common tasks. Exact widgets depend on how your admins configured the app.

Where is my member profile?

Open your account from the user menu (often your name or avatar). Your profile shows contact details, membership dates, training, RFIDs you are allowed to use, payments history (if exposed), and other fields your organization tracks.

How do I update my email, phone, or address?

Edit your profile from the member area your admins pointed you to. Some fields may sync from your login provider; if a field is read-only or keeps reverting, ask an admin to update it at the source or in Member Manager.

What are RFIDs, and how do I add one?

RFID tags or cards are often used for door access. If self-service is enabled, you can register a tag from the RFID section of your profile. Follow any on-screen instructions (tap the reader, confirm a code, and so on). If you do not see that option, contact an admin to enroll your tag.

How does membership status work?

Your profile shows whether you are active, inactive, or in another state the space defines. Status usually comes from payments and admin approvals. If you believe it is wrong, check recent payments first, then ask billing or membership volunteers.

Where do I see payments or renew?

Many spaces sync PayPal, Recharge, Ko-Fi, or similar. Your profile may list recorded payments. Renewal links and policies are set by your organization—not every payment method appears inside Member Manager.

What is training tracking?

Some tools or rooms require training. Your profile can list topics you are cleared on and who trained you. To get trained, follow your space’s process (class, checkout, mentor sign-off); admins record completion in the system.

How do I apply for membership?

If your space uses the built-in application flow, use the apply link they gave you (often from the public site). You may need to verify your email before starting. Otherwise your space might use an external form—use whatever they publish.

What is in the Help menu?

Under Help you may see General (overview and policies written by your admins) and FAQ (this kind of page). Only administrators see Admin FAQ.

How does search work?

Use the search box in the navigation to find members or records your role is allowed to see. You only get results you are permitted to view.

Who can I contact if I’m stuck?

Use the contact information your organization publishes (email, Slack, phone). Member Manager does not replace those channels—it’s the tool they use behind the scenes.
